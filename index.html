About Me

I am a Marketing Professional who believes great marketing starts with understanding people — shaped by 4+ years of real-world customer experience in the F&B industry.

I specialize in digital content creation, social media strategy, and campaign execution — with proven results including a 1.5M-view TikTok campaign. My toolkit includes Photoshop, Canva, OBS Studio, AI content tools, Advanced Excel (VBA/Macros), Google Analytics, and CRM systems.

My marketing approach is shaped by both creative and business perspectives — combining a Diploma in Marketing Management with an ABE-Endorsed Diploma in International Business Administration (UK) from MIBA University. This dual foundation allows me to see marketing not just as content and campaigns, but as a business function that drives growth, manages customer relationships, and delivers measurable ROI. Further sharpened by a Content Writing Certificate from Strategy First College (SFUx) and a Graphic Design Certificate from Skill Plus Training Center.

I don’t just create — I think in strategy, speak in data, and connect through people.
